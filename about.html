This site was created to support CS education.

It attempts to offer students and teachers ideas and inspiration to experiment with Computer Science topics outside those generally presented in CS education circles. Computers, programming languages, algorithms, data structures, and various combinations of each offer infinite opportunities for creativity, fun, and real world-changing innovation. This site's ultimate goal is to expose learners to these topics from as many weird angles as possible. The posts here are intended to act as starting points for full excursions into the CS concepts they present.

Click, explore, think, and think more.
